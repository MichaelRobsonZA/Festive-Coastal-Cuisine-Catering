About Our Catering Service

The story behind our passion for coastal cuisine

Our Story

Festive Coastal Cuisine Catering was founded in 2015 by Chef Maria Coastal, who grew up in a small fishing village where fresh seafood was a way of life. After training at top culinary schools and working in renowned restaurants along both coasts, Maria developed a vision for a catering service that would bring the authentic flavors of coastal cuisine to events of all sizes.

Starting with just a small team of passionate culinary experts, Festive Coastal Cuisine quickly gained a reputation for exceptional food quality and presentation. Today, we are proud to serve the entire region with a expanded team of chefs, event planners, and service staff who share our dedication to creating memorable dining experiences.

Our journey has been guided by a commitment to sustainability, supporting local suppliers, and honoring traditional coastal recipes while embracing innovative culinary techniques.

Chef Maria preparing for one of our first catered events in 2015

Our Philosophy

At Festive Coastal Cuisine, we believe that exceptional catering begins with the finest ingredients. Our culinary philosophy centers around three core principles:

Freshness First

We source the freshest seafood and produce available, working with local fishermen and farmers whenever possible. Our ingredients are delivered daily, ensuring peak quality and flavor in every dish.

Sustainable Practices

We are committed to environmentally responsible practices, from sourcing sustainable seafood to minimizing waste through careful planning and eco-friendly packaging and service materials.

Passion in Every Plate

We believe that food made with passion tastes better. Our team approaches each event with creativity, attention to detail, and a genuine love for culinary artistry that shines through in every dish we serve.
