About Us

Bringing the fresh flavors of the coast to your special events.

At Festive Coastal Cuisine Catering, we combine the bounty of the sea with locally-sourced ingredients to create memorable dining experiences. Founded in 2015 by Chef Maria Coastal, our team of culinary experts is dedicated to providing exceptional food and service for any occasion.

Our philosophy is simple: use the freshest ingredients, prepare them with care, and serve them with style. Whether you're planning an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, we bring the coastal experience to you.
